About the Association

Deoria Taekwondo Association trains athletes in Modern-style Taekwondo with focus on discipline, fitness, confidence and mental strength.

Our association operates with the vision of creating strong, respectful and high-performing martial artists capable of competing at district, state, national and international levels.

Sparring (Kyorugi) — Olympic competition format
Poomsae Forms — precision, balance & discipline
Self Defense — real-world practical techniques
Flexibility & Fitness conditioning
Competition Training & mental toughness

Path to Glory

The Championship Journey

01

District Championships

Begin competing in local and district level competitions. Build your foundation, timing, and match experience against peers.

02

State Championships

Selected athletes advance to state competitions, testing skills against top talents from across Uttar Pradesh.

03

National Championships

Compete in SGFI, CBSE Nationals and Federation events — representing Deoria on the national stage.

04

International Events

Earn world ranking points through WT sanctioned competitions and continental tournaments.

05

Olympic Qualification

Represent India in Olympic qualification tournaments and aim for the ultimate dream — the Olympic Games.
